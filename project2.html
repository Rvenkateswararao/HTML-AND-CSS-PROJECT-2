Sergery We Provide's

Brain Sergery

Craniotomy. A piece of the skull is removed to give doctors access to the brain to remove a brain tumor, abnormal tissue, blood, or blood clots; relieve pressure after an injury or stroke; repair a brain aneurysm or skull fractures; or treat other brain conditions.

Eye Sergery

LASIK eye surgery is the best known and most commonly performed laser refractive surgery to correct vision problems. Laser-assisted in situ keratomileusis (LASIK) can be an alternative to glasses or contact lenses.

Stomach Sergery
